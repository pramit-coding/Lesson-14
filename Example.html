History of Cricket

Cricket originated in southeast England in the middle of the 16th century. By the end of the 18th century, it had developed into the national sport of England.

The game of cricket has a known history spanning from the 16th century to the present day, with international matches having been played since 1844.

Basic Rules of Cricket

  1. Two teams of eleven players each.
  2. The game is played on a circular or oval-shaped field.
  3. The main objective is to score more runs than the opposing team.
  4. Runs are scored by hitting the ball and running between the wickets or by hitting boundaries.
  5. The game is divided into innings where one team bats and the other bowls and fields.
